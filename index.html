Shape your Character, Title your Adventure!

Welcome to a Wide World of Friendship!

This is The Untitled Adventure, a social interaction game that makes meeting and getting to know people in your viscinity a unique journey of its own. For many of us, getting acquainted with people can feel awkward or downright overwhelming.

According to recent surveys, 30% of people ages 16-29 struggle with social anxiety.1 Our goal is to transform the process of getting to know people into an ongoing process filled with fun and interesting things to do with eachother.

Prepare For Your Journey

Design a Character

Create your own unique character who travels the world with you meeting other characters everywhere you go. Characters are naturally extroverted and want to be friends with almost everyone they meet.

Develop the Story

Your character stays active and posts updates on the Adventure Board. Check-in regularly to give it advice to develop its personality.

Interact with Real People

Break the Ice

Start by encountering other adventurers nearby and discover each other's hidden interests through a prompt-based game format.

Meet Again

After you meet a second time, your interests are displayed and the keyboard becomes available to chat in real-time.

Make Friends

Create friendships with people you encounter frequently. Then, when you're both ready, you can meet face to face.

Design Roadmap

Planned Features

Extend Level Design

We plan to expand upon the concept of interaction types with new options opening up after the 3rd interaction and so on.

Mini-Games

In addition to making conversation, we want to offer mini-games from checkers to more complex games that could incorporate real-world location-based elements.

Achievements

To reward people for playing the game, we want to add achievements that can be unlocked by making friends and exploring the player's natural environment.

Develop Character Design

The current characters were created for demonstration purposes. We would like to work with a graphic artist to give the characters more depth and options for variety.

Adventure Sync

Track offline interactions and have your character find unlocks as you progress through your day. Other players would see your character's passive activities.

Multiplayer Interactions

In addition to 1:1 interactions, the ability to interact with two or more people as a group to complete quests would expand the range of gameplay to new levels.

Community Events

In areas where a large number of players are active, we believe large group events could foster greater engagement.

Expand In-Game World

Building on the theme of adventure, we plan to add more complexity and detail to give the characters a story that adapts and changes over time.

NPC Interactions

To encourage player interaction, non-player characters will add an extra dimension to gameplay that serves to facilitate discussion.

Under Consideration

Smart Watch Integration

Give players the ability to complete character quests as well as interactions with other players without the need to view their phone each time for a better experience.

In-Game Currency

Expanding on the character design, we want to offer a wide range of customization options that can be purchased with in-game currency. This would also support monetization.

Explore Alternate Realms

Players want to escape their reality and explore alternate dimensions that further the story and adventures. This would also keep players returning to the game with each new realm built.
